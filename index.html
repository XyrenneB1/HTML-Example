Minecarft - The High Selling Vidoe Game of All Time

Minecraft is an open-world sandbox game — meaning it gives players a procedurally generated, virtually limitless world made of blocky “voxels” where they can mine resources, craft items, build structures, explore, and survive. Some of its features are: • Creative Mode: Unlimited blocks and freedom to build anything. • Survival Mode: Players gather resources and face monsters. • Multiplayer: Join friends or communities online. • Modding & Custom Content: Powerful community tools and mods. • Educational Use: Used in schools for subjects like math and design.
